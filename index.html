ABOUT MATTIE

Mattie is a native of Ruckersville, VA. After graduating from William Monroe High School, he received a BA in Communications from Virginia Tech. Following that graduation, he attended Air Force ROTC at the University of Virginia was commissioned as a Second Lieutenant in the United States Air Force, where he served for 5 years as a Public Affairs officer.

After leaving the Air Force, Mattie went to work in the insurance industry for Liberty Mutual, and shortly after that decided to start his own independent insurance agency, The Fuller Agency. He operated this business for 8 years, eventually selling it to focus on Real Estate and his church.

Mattie got his real estate license in 2014 and has been actively assisting buyers and sellers in the Central Virginia area since that time. He is proudly affiliated with one of the fastest growing brokerages in the country, eXp Realty. In addition to real estate, Mattie is passionate about music, and you can often catch him playing gigs at many of the local wineries and breweries as well as leading worship for Legacy Church in Ruckersville.

Mattie and his wife Amy live in Ruckersville with their 3 children, Parker, Avery and Mallory.
